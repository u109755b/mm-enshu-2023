Ned Land, Captain Nemo, assume attitude towards coco-nuts, And I do not think, said the Canadian, that he would object to our introducing a cargo of coco-nuts on board.

I, Conseil, express concern, I might perhaps wake one day to find myself half devoured.
Conseil, Ned Land, question trust, I would not trust you.
Ned Land, Birds of paradise, longed to catch, Nor I, Ned, if I do not catch a bird of paradise.
Ned Land, Nautilus, propose not returning, Suppose we do not return to the Nautilus this evening? said Conseil.
