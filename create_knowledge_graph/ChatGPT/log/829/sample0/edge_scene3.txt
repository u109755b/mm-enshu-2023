Emperor of Lilliput, Author, visit, The emperor of Lilliput comes to see the author in his confinement.
Emperor of Lilliput, Learned men of Lilliput, appoint, Learned men appointed to teach the author their language.
Soldiers, Author, take from, His pockets are searched, and his sword and pistols taken from him by soldiers.
Cooks and Butlers of Lilliput, Author, give victuals and drink, He ordered his cooks and butlers to give me victuals and drink.
Emperor of Lilliput, Ladies in attendance, follow, The empress, and young princes of the blood of both sexes, attended by many ladies.
Emperor of Lilliput, Attendants, command, The emperor had his attendants run in and held the bridle.
Emperor of Lilliput, Cooks and Butlers of Lilliput, order, He ordered his cooks and butlers, who were already prepared, to give me victuals and drink.
Emperor of Lilliput, Guards, leave with, I was left with a strong guard.
Colonel, Ringleaders, order to seize, But the colonel ordered six of the ringleaders to be seized.
Colonel, Author, deliver, Colonel thought no punishment so proper as to deliver the ringleaders bound into my hands.
