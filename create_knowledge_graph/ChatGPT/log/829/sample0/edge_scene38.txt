Author, Master, have room made for; notifies of departure; admits to conversations; watches departure; gives token of distinction to, My master had ordered a room to be made for me; The author has notice given him by his master, that he must depart from the country; He was known frequently to converse with me; But his honour, out of curiosity...was determined to see me in my canoe; ...he did me the honour to raise it gently to my mouth.
Author, Sorrel Nag, helped by; consults with, I had worked two chairs with my knife, the sorrel nag helping me; I returned home, and consulting with the sorrel nag.
