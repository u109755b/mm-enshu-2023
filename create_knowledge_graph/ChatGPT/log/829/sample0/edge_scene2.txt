Author, Father of the Author, beget, My father had a small estate in Nottinghamshire; I was the third of five sons.
Father of the Author, Author, provide education, He sent me to Emanuel College in Cambridge at fourteen years old.
Author, Mr. James Bates, apprenticed, I was bound apprentice to Mr. James Bates, an eminent surgeon in London.
Father of the Author, Author, provide financial support, My father now and then sending me small sums of money.
Author, Uncle John, provide financial support, by the assistance of him and my uncle John, and some other relations, I got forty pounds.
Mr. James Bates, Author, recommend, Soon after my return from Leyden, I was recommended by my good master, Mr. Bates, to be surgeon to the Swallow.
Captain Abraham Pannel, Author, employ, I was surgeon to the Swallow, Captain Abraham Pannel, commander.
Author, Mrs. Mary Burton, marry, I married Mrs. Mary Burton, second daughter to Mr. Edmund Burton.
Mr. Edmund Burton, Author, be father-in-law, I married Mrs. Mary Burton, second daughter to Mr. Edmund Burton.
Captain William Prichard, Author, employ, I accepted an advantageous offer from Captain William Prichard, master of the Antelope.
Author, Lilliputians, made prisoner by, is made a prisoner, and carried up the country.
Lilliputians, Author, carry, is made a prisoner, and carried up the country.
Author, The Emperor of Lilliput, send provisions, plenty of meat and drink should be sent to me.
The Emperor of Lilliput, Author, order tying up, the emperor had early notice of it by an express; and determined in council that I should be tied.
The Emperor of Lilliput, Author, prepare transportation, and a machine prepared to carry me to the capital city.
