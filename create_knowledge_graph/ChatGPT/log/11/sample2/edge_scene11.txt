Alice, Jury, tip over, Alice tipped over the jury-box with the edge of her skirt.
Alice, Lizard, misplace & assist, Alice had put the Lizard in head downwards in the jury-box and later assisted by getting it out and putting it right.
King, Alice, address, “The trial cannot proceed,” said the King to Alice.
King, White Rabbit, correct, The King hastily said unimportant after the White Rabbit corrected him.
Queen, Alice, declare, “Nearly two miles high,” added the Queen to Alice.
Alice, Queen, refuse, Alice said to the Queen, “Well, I shan't go, at any rate."
Alice, Alice's Sister, narrate, Alice told her sister about her strange Adventures.
