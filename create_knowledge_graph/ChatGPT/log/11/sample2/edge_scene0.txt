Alice, Alice's Sister, sit by & have boredom, Alice was sitting by her sister on the bank and was beginning to get very tired of having nothing to do.
Alice, White Rabbit, see & follow & pursue, Alice saw, followed, and continued pursuing the White Rabbit with pink eyes that ran close by her.
White Rabbit, Alice, be seen by & be pursued by, The White Rabbit was seen by and pursued by Alice.
Alice, Dinah, think of, Alice thought of Dinah and hoped she would not miss her.
Dinah, Alice, be thought of, Dinah was thought of by Alice during her fall.
