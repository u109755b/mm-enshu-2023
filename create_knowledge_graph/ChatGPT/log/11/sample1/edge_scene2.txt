Alice, Lory, argue & claim superiority, Alice had an argument with Lory; Lory claimed to be older and know better.
Mouse, Everyone, call to attention & request more stories, The Mouse called everyone to attention and also was asked by everyone to tell more stories.
Dodo, Everyone, suggest adjournment & explain Caucus-race, Dodo suggested adjourning the meeting and explained the Caucus-race.
Everyone, Alice, distribute prizes & demand prizes, Alice distributed prizes and everyone demanded them from her.
Old Crab, Young Crab, give advice & retort, Old Crab gave advice to Young Crab; Young Crab retorted.
