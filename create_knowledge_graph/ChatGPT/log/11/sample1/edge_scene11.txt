Alice, The Jurymen, tip over, Alice tipped over the jury-box, upsetting all the jurymen.
Alice, The Lizard (Bill), put right, Alice having put the Lizard in head downwards, she soon got him out again and put him right.
The King, Alice, look at, The King looking hard at Alice and said the trial cannot proceed until all the jurymen are back in their proper places.
The King, Alice, question, The King said to Alice, “What do you know about this business?”
The King, Alice, ordering out, The King read out from his book that all persons more than a mile high to leave the court and looked at Alice.
The Queen, Alice, add information, The Queen added that Alice was nearly two miles high.
Alice, The King, contradict, Alice contradicted The King's rule saying it’s not a regular rule and invented just now.
The Knave, The King, deny, The Knave denied writing the letter to The King.
The King, The Knave, accuse, If you didn’t sign it, said the King, that only makes the matter worse.
The Queen, The Knave, conclude, The Queen concluded that this proves his guilt.
Alice, The Queen and The King, rebuke, Alice rebuked Queen and King's conclusion saying they don’t even know what the verses are about.
The Lizard (Bill), The Queen, be hit, The Queen furiously threw an inkstand at The Lizard while speaking.
Alice, Alice's Sister, narrate, Alice told her sister all these strange Adventures.
Alice's Sister, Alice, advice, Alice's Sister kissed her and said “It was a curious dream, dear, certainly: but now run in to your tea; it’s getting late.”
