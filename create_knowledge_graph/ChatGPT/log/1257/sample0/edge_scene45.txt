Cardinal Richelieu, Musketeers, enter, The cardinal opened the door of the room in which the Musketeers were.
Porthos, Aramis, play, Porthos playing an earnest game of dice with Aramis.
Cardinal Richelieu, Athos, inquire, "What has become of Monseigneur Athos?" asked the cardinal.
Porthos, Athos, explain, Porthos explained Athos has gone as a scout.
Cardinal Richelieu, Porthos, inquire, "And you, what have you done, Monsieur Porthos?"
Porthos, Aramis, win, "I have won five pistoles of Aramis," said Porthos.
Cardinal Richelieu, Musketeers, order, "To horse, then, gentlemen; for it is getting late," commanded the cardinal.
Cardinal Richelieu, Duke of Buckingham, engage (assassination), Milady made an engagement with Cardinal Richelieu to cause the Duke of Buckingham to be assassinated.
