Four adventurers, Paris, leave from, Our four adventurers left Paris by the Barrière St. Denis.
The Musketeers, horses, have, The black horses of the Musketeers.
The lackeys, The Musketeers, follow, The lackeys followed, armed to the teeth.
Porthos, The stranger (Gentleman), proposed a toast to, The stranger proposed to Porthos to drink the health of the cardinal.
Athos, Porthos, instructed to, You have committed a piece of folly, said Athos, but it can’t be helped; there is no drawing back. Kill the fellow, and rejoin us as soon as you can.
Laborers, travelers, insulted, The laborers began to jeer the travelers and by their insolence disturbed the equanimity even of Athos.
Aramis, laborers, criticized, Aramis, not liking to soil his boots with this artificial mortar, apostrophized them rather sharply.
Mousqueton, Aramis, was wounded like, Aramis received a ball which passed through his shoulder, and Mousqueton another ball which lodged in the fleshy part.
Bazin, Aramis, left with, They lifted him off at the door of a cabaret, left Bazin with him.
Athos, promises, makes, “Morbleu,” said Athos, “reduced to two masters and Grimaud and Planchet! Morbleu! I won’t be their dupe, I will answer for it."
