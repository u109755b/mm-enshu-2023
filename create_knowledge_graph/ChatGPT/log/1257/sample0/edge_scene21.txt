Duke of Buckingham, D’Artagnan, endeavor to draw information & surprised, The duke endeavored to draw from D’Artagnan what had happened and was surprised that he had not been arrested by the cardinal.
Host of the inn at St. Valery, D’Artagnan, horse and route instruction, The host provided D’Artagnan with a horse and instructions.
Landlord of the Golden Harrow, D’Artagnan, provides horse, The landlord of the Golden Harrow furnished a horse for D’Artagnan.
M. de Tréville, D’Artagnan, reception and instruction, M. de Tréville received D’Artagnan and directed him to his duty post.
