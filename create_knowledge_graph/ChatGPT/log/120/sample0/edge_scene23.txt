Coracle, Spy-glass, placed behind, The sun was still hid from me behind the great bulk of the Spy-glass.
Coracle, Sea lions, observed by, I beheld huge slimy monsters--soft snails, as it were, of incredible bigness--two or three score of them together, which I understood later to be sea lions.
Coracle, HISPANIOLA, sighted by, Right in front of me, not half a mile away, I beheld the HISPANIOLA under sail.
Captain Smollett, HISPANIOLA, associated with, I could return the vessel to her captain, Captain Smollett.
