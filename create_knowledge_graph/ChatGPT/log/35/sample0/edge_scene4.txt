I, Fragile thing out of futurity, face to face, In another moment we were standing face to face, I and this fragile thing out of futurity.
Exquisite creatures, I, addressed, One of them addressed me.
I, Little people, saw confidence in, Indeed, there was something in these pretty little people that inspired confidence.
I, Little people, could fling, I could fancy myself flinging the whole dozen of them about like ninepins.
I, Little people, made a motion to warn, But I made a sudden motion to warn them when I saw their little pink hands feeling at the Time Machine.
Fair-haired little creature, I, followed gesture, At once a quaintly pretty little figure in chequered purple and white followed my gesture.
Fair-haired little creature, I, imitated thunder, and then astonished me by imitating the sound of thunder.
Fair-haired little creature, I, asked a question, Then one of them suddenly asked me a question that showed him to be on the intellectual level of one of our five-year-old children.
I, Fair-haired little creature, gave vivid rendering, I nodded, pointed to the sun, and gave them such a vivid rendering of a thunderclap as startled them.
Fair-haired little creature, I, brought flowers, Then came one laughing towards me, carrying a chain of beautiful flowers altogether new to me, and put it about my neck.
Little people, I, dined with, There were, perhaps, a couple of hundred people dining in the hall, and most of them, seated as near to me as they could come, were watching me with interest.
I, Little people, attempted to learn speech, I determined to make a resolute attempt to learn the speech of these new men of mine.
